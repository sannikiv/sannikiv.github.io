**Postdoctoral Researcher at the Department of Economics, Faculty of Business and Economics, University of Lausanne.**

**Senior Researcher at the Labour Institute for Economic Research Labore.**

I received my doctoral degree in Economics from the University of Jyväskylä in Finland.

During my doctoral studies I visited the University of Melbourne.

I do empirical research using microdata. My research interests are in labor and public economics, with a special interest in topics related to migration, gender, and equality.
